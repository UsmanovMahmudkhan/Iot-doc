Project Overview

Transforming mobility for the visually impaired community

Problem Statement

Over 285 million people worldwide are visually impaired. Traditional navigation aids like white canes have limitations - they only detect obstacles at ground level and require constant manual operation. Our Smart Blind Shoes provide hands-free, comprehensive obstacle detection.

Solution

An intelligent footwear system with ultrasonic sensors, haptic feedback, and real-time web monitoring. The shoes detect obstacles in all directions and provide intuitive vibration patterns to guide users safely.

Key Benefits

  • Hands-free navigation
  • 360¬∞ obstacle detection
  • Real-time monitoring via web dashboard
  • Emergency alert system
  • Step counting and distance tracking
